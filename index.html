Kachori Gali, Varanasi

Kachori Gali is a narrow street in Varanasi, India, that is home to a plethora of kachori shops. Kachoris are a type of fried snack made with dough and a variety of fillings, and they are a popular street food in India. The kachoris in Kachori Gali are some of the best in the world, and they are a must-try for any visitor to Varanasi.
